Scoring Dimensions (Track 02 AMA)

Repayment Capacity

Disposable income, DTI, salary stability, txn-inferred income, multi-bank holistic view.

Purchase Intent

Loan page visits, EMI calculator usage, session depth, application started, window-shopping flags.

Behavioral Discipline

Day-1 salary spend, need vs luxury mix, savings transfers, bureau enquiries.

Product Match

Home, Mortgage, Auto, Personal, Consumer Durable — gated by affordable EMI estimate.
