Compliance & Governance

  • DPDP: Customer consent via AA framework; data minimization for scoring only.
  • No auto-credit-decision: AI tiers and briefs assist RMs; underwriter retains final authority.
  • Human-in-loop: ML nudge capped at ±8 pts; Quality Leads never demoted by model.
  • RBI AI norms: Explainability on every lead — rule reasons + ML contributions + audit trail.
  • RM access control: PIN/session gate on demo; production maps to IDBI SSO/LDAP.
